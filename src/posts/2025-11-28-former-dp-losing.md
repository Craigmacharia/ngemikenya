The setback exposes fractures in his DCP-led coalition, weakening Mt Kenya influence and stalling mass resignations. Barred from running by impeachment rules, Gachagua eyes a 2027 anti-Ruto front with allies like Kalonzo Musyoka, but analysts warn poor momentum risks relegating him to political irrelevance.
