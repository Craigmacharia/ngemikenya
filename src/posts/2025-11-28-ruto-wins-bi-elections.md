In a resounding victory for President William Ruto's administration, the United Democratic Alliance (UDA) and allied Orange Democratic Movement (ODM) secured the majority of seats in Kenya's November 27, 2025 by-elections. Key wins included UDA's Joseph Ndakwa in Malava, James Wamuthende in Kimilili, and ODM's Emmanuel Chemitei in Ainabkoi, bolstering Ruto's political influence ahead of 2027. Ruto hailed the results, stating, "We are bigger and better when united," amid shifting alliances and surprise outcomes. (68 words)
